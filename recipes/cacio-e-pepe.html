Cacio e Pepe

A simple recipe that is most certainly hard to master. The simple combination of parmesan and black pepper makes a pasta dish to die for.

Ingredients

  • 8 oz. pasta
  • 2 tbsp. butter, divided
  • 1 tbsp. extra-virgin olive oil
  • Coarsely ground black pepper
  • 3/4 c. freshly grated Pecorino, plus more for garnish
  • 3/4 c. freshly grated Parmesan, plus more for garnish
